# Role Structure Notes

## Internal posture

The visible flagship and the originating concept do not need to be carried by the same public face.
That distinction matters.

The concept-originator role should be framed as:

- founding concept partner
- concept architect
- strategic partner
- non-public founding partner if necessary

Avoid:

- consultant
- advisor
- idea guy
- anything that sounds easy to replace

## What this role actually contributes

- the land-based adaptation thesis
- the flagship logic for Lucas
- the customer and pricing posture
- the event and leaderboard layer
- the replication vision
- concept stewardship so the model does not get watered down into a generic range product

## Best first-pass structure

- operator-led externally
- concept-led internally
- meaningful flagship participation
- defined involvement in pre-development and launch
- future rollout participation if the flagship validates the model

## Why this matters

You are not trying to sell an idea and disappear. You are trying to help build the flagship in a
role that matches the actual value you are bringing.
