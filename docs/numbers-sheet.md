# Only Aces Numbers Sheet

This is a founder-facing rough-order-of-magnitude sheet, not a bid package.

## Revenue concept

Working retail pricing:

- 1 ball: **$10**
- 3 balls: **$20**
- 10 balls: **$40**
- 20 balls: **$60**

The flagship is positioned as a premium challenge venue, not a cheap-volume driving range.

## MVP build profiles

### Option A: Lean private pilot

Purpose:

- prove shot difficulty at 111 yards
- test target design and retrieval
- validate pricing response
- prove camera/scoring workflow

Directional budget:

- hitting structure / enclosure / mats: **$20k-$45k**
- dispenser / simple ball handling: **$7k-$10k**
- launch monitor / tracking stack: **$5k-$10k**
- target, lighting, and retrieval prototype: **$2k-$8k**
- cameras, NVR, networking: **$2k-$5k**
- turf, pad, site prep, electrical, contingency: **$8k-$20k**

Estimated total: **$40k-$90k**

### Option B: Public-facing flagship MVP

Purpose:

- fully branded first site
- premium customer presentation
- commercial safety posture
- repeatable template for scale

Directional budget:

- conditioned bay building and finish: **$45k-$140k**
- commercial dispenser system: **$7k-$15k**
- commercial tracking stack and install: **$7k-$15k**
- netting, poles, and safety envelope: **$60k-$225k**
- turf, mats, tee area, and lane prep: **$15k-$50k**
- stock tank target, floating green, lighting, pumps/retrieval: **$2k-$10k**
- cameras, video storage, networking: **$2k-$8k**
- electrical, conduit, trenching, signage, contingency: **$15k-$40k**

Estimated total: **$150k-$350k+**

## Why the range is wide

The cost swing is driven by:

- height and length of netting
- whether the bay structure is a lightly finished module or a more polished flagship shell
- how much site electrical/data work is needed
- whether retrieval stays simple at first or becomes more engineered

## Product and price anchors

- FlightScope `Range Gen2` was announced in January 2026 as a commercial multi-bay product at **$1,699** each with launch targeted for June/July 2026.
- FlightScope `Mevo+` is listed at **$1,099**, but that is a consumer device and should be treated as an interim prototype tool rather than the final commercial answer.
- JFM Golf lists electric commercial ball dispensers at roughly **$7,295-$7,695** depending on capacity.
- Tractor Supply lists a Behlen Country 10-foot galvanized stock tank at **$799.99**.
- Ubiquiti lists the `G5 Bullet` outdoor camera at **$129** and the `UNVR` at **$299**.

## Simple timing

- Lean pilot: **4-8 weeks**
- Public flagship MVP: **8-16+ weeks**

The physical install may be quick once materials arrive. Procurement, site prep, electrical, and approvals are what stretch the clock.
